Privacy Policy

We respect your data. Here's exactly what we collect and what we don't.

Effective: March 2026
I

What We Collect

When you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. When you submit an event suggestion or contact us, we collect your email and message content. That's it.

✦ ✦ ✦
II

What We Don't Collect

We do not use cookies for tracking. We do not use Google Analytics. We do not sell, share, or trade your personal information with third parties. We do not build user profiles. We do not track your browsing behavior within the site.

✦ ✦ ✦
III

Analytics

We use Vercel's built-in analytics, which collects aggregated, anonymized page view data. No personal information is collected. No cookies are set. This is compliant with GDPR without requiring a cookie consent banner.

✦ ✦ ✦
IV

Third-Party Services

Email subscriptions and contact forms are processed through FormSubmit.co. Donations are processed through Stripe. Both services have their own privacy policies. We do not store payment information.

✦ ✦ ✦
V

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your email from our records at any time by contacting contact.cosmosdaily@gmail.com. We will comply within 48 hours.
